Scenario Notes

  • Ballistic missiles are easy to detect but can saturate defenses.
  • Hypersonic gliders are fast, violent, and difficult to intercept.
  • Cruise missiles and drones fly low, threatening radar and cities.
  • Drone swarms can blind the radar net and waste expensive interceptors.
  • Fighters are strongest against drones and cruise missiles, but only if the airbase survives.
